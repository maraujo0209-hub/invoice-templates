Independent Contractor Agreement

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

This Agreement is entered into by and between Your Service Company ("Company") and Apex Subcontracting Services LLC ("Contractor").

1. Services Provided

Contractor agrees to perform commercial site cleaning services as requested by the Company on a project-by-project basis in accordance with site specifications provided.

2. Compensation

The Company shall pay Contractor a flat rate or hourly fee agreed upon prior to project commencement. Payments shall be disbursed within 14 days of invoice submission.

3. Relationship of Parties

The Contractor is an independent service provider and not an employee of the Company. Contractor retains full responsibility for their taxes, licensing, and insurance.
