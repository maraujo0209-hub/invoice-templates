Contractor Agreement

Agreement Date: July 21, 2026

1. Services & Scope

The Contractor agrees to perform the services detailed in attached Work Orders or statements of work in a professional and timely manner.

2. Confidentiality

The Contractor shall maintain strict confidentiality regarding all non-public business operations, client lists, and proprietary procedures learned during engagement.

3. Ownership of Work

All work product, materials, and deliverables produced under this agreement belong exclusively to the Company upon payment in full.

4. Expenses & Compensation

Contractor will be compensated at agreed project or hourly rates. Pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses will be reimbursed upon submitting valid receipts.

5. Termination & Liability

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice. Contractor remains liable for maintaining appropriate commercial liability insurance during active work.

6. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This agreement is governed by state laws. Disputes will first attempt resolution through good-faith negotiation before formal mediation.
