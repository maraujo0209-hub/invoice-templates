CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2026-07-21

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is made between Your Business Name ("Company") and Contractor Name / Business ("Contractor").

1. Services Rendered

Contractor agrees to perform commercial services as outlined in applicable work orders.

2. Term & Termination

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either party with 14 days written notice.

3. Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is an independent party and not an employee. Contractor is responsible for all applicable taxes and insurance.

4. Confidentiality & Ownership of Work

Contractor shall maintain strict confidentiality. All work product developed belongs exclusively to Company.

5. Compensation & Expenses

Payment shall be made within 15 days of invoice submission. Contractor covers all self-incurred business expenses.

6. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State where Company operates. Disputes shall be settled via binding arbitration.
