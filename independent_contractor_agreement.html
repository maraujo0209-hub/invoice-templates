Independent Contractor Agreement

Document Ref: ICA-0001
Effective Date: 2026-07-12

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into and made effective as of the date written above, by and between the following parties:

Company: [Your Company Name], located at [123 Business Street, Suite 100, City, State, Zip Code] ("Client").

Contractor: [Independent Contractor / Entity Name], located at [Contractor Street Address, City, State, Zip Code] ("Contractor").

1. Services to be Performed

Contractor agrees to perform the specialized professional consulting, development, operational design, or deployment services as outlined explicitly in corresponding statement of work addendums authorized by both parties.

2. Compensation & Terms

The Client agrees to compensate the Contractor according to the fixed milestones or hourly rate parameter structures explicitly detailed in accepted project terms. Contractor is solely responsible for processing all personal or corporate income taxes, withholdings, and operational overhead expenditures.

3. Independent Contractor Status

The parties intend that Contractor’s relationship to Client under this Agreement is that of an independent contractor. Neither party has the authority to bind or obligate the other in any manner whatsoever. Contractor is not entitled to any medical, holiday, retirement, or operational insurance benefits provided by the Client.

4. Intellectual Property & Confidentiality

All work products, digital designs, code components, scripts, or operational collateral produced by the Contractor under this contract belong exclusively to the Client upon successful compensation distribution. Contractor agrees to safeguard all proprietary system metadata, code repositories, or operational workflows accessed during execution.

5. Termination

Either party may terminate this active arrangement upon providing [14] calendar days written notice to the other party. In the event of early termination, Client shall compensate Contractor for all verified milestones successfully completed up to the termination effective date.
