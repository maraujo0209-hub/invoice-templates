How to Use Your Templates

01

Replace the Branding Placeholder

Locate the standard 300x150 gray box at the top of your document. Delete it and upload your corporate or personal business logo image in its place.

02

Add Your Permanent Company Details

Input your company name, physical address, phone numbers, tax IDs, and digital payment links into the primary configuration areas.

03

Save Your Master Copy

Save this personalized file cleanly as your Master Copy. Do not edit this file directly for future daily transactions.

04

Duplicate for New Transactions

Whenever a client requires a new invoice, estimate, or receipt, open your master copy and select "Save As" or "Duplicate". Update the serial identifier digits (e.g., changing INV-0001 to INV-0002) along with the specific line items.

05

Export as PDF Before Sending

Once editing is complete, always export or print your final document safely as a static PDF file. This locks your formatting securely in place before transmission to your customer.
